Hello there and welcome to my personal webpage, created by yours truly!

My name is Jacqueline Wilson and I am a 22-year-old Telecommunication: Media and Society Senior attending the University of Florida.

I absolutely love the educational and social aspects of the university as well as the beautiful campus and of course, our football team! GO GATORS!

My major is now Telecommunications with a focus in Media and Society. During the summer of 2013 I completed an internship with The Independent Florida Alligator where I worked with the advertising department. I plan on completing my bachelors degree in the spring of 2014 and from then on out the future is wide open for me! I would love to travel a little more before beginning either a career or graduate school. In a perfect world I would love to eventually work in the new media field in Miami.
